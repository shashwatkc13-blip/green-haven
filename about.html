Our Story

A Passion for Plants

Founded by plant enthusiast Rashmi Chhetri, Green Haven began with a simple mission: to share the joy and tranquility of living with plants. What started as a personal passion in the beautiful city of Dehradun has blossomed into a cherished local business.

Our philosophy is rooted in quality and care. We believe that every plant has a story and a unique personality. We source the healthiest, most beautiful plants and provide our customers with the knowledge and support they need to help them thrive.

From our humble beginnings, Green Haven has grown into a community hub for plant lovers, dedicated to making Dehradun a greener, more beautiful place, one plant at a time.
